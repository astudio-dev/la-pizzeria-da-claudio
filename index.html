Est. on the island — made in Italy

From Claudio's oven,
with amore

Italian owned and operated, La Pizzeria brings genuine traditional cooking to Koh Tao. Our dough rises slowly, our sauce is crushed from real Italian tomatoes, and our cold cuts — pancetta, coppa, mortadella — are cured in-house from scratch.

Thirty-one pizzas. Handmade lasagne, gnocchi and ravioli. Cannoli filled to order with homemade ricotta. And a wine list that flew 9,000 km to be here.
